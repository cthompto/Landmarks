Landmarks




I am plagued by a question.









When confronted by facial recognition.




As a trans person, is it better to be seen accurately?




Or not seen at all...









How does the algorithm see me? How does it guess what I'm feeling?





Is there such a thing as a neutral expression? Can it tell the difference between a smile caused by a joke and one that masks rage?





Do I want it to be able to tell the difference?
